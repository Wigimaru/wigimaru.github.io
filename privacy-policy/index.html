Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Wigimaru (“Wigimaru,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) develops and publishes mobile games (collectively, the “Games”). This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use our Games, why it may be processed, and what choices you may have.

Information We Collect

You do not need to create an account or provide your name, email address, or other contact information to play our Games. Wigimaru does not directly collect or store personal information through registration forms, user profiles, or player accounts.

However, our Games integrate third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) for advertising, consent management, analytics, crash reporting, and technical functionality. These third-party services may automatically collect or process information such as:

  • device type, model, hardware specifications, and operating system version;
  • device or app identifiers, including the Google Advertising ID (AAID) where permitted;
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it (country or region);
  • language, time zone, and general device network settings;
  • information about interactions with the Game, such as sessions, gameplay events, and in-game achievements;
  • advertisement impressions, clicks, views, and related performance attribution data;
  • crash reports, diagnostic information, stack traces, and technical logs; and
  • consent choices, strings, and privacy preferences.

Wigimaru does not use this information to determine your real-world identity.

How Information Is Used

Information processed through our Games may be used to:

  • operate, support, and maintain the underlying game functions;
  • display and measure advertisements, including personalized advertisements where permitted;
  • manage advertising and privacy consent choices via Google's User Messaging Platform;
  • analyze aggregate usage trends to optimize the app;
  • identify and resolve bugs, crashes, errors, and technical stability problems;
  • detect fraud, invalid ad traffic, or abusive activity; and
  • comply with applicable legal and platform obligations.
Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), our legal bases for processing include:
  • Consent: Where required, for personalized advertising, analytics, or storing and accessing information on a device (managed through the in-game consent screen built on Google UMP).
  • Legitimate Interest: For crash reporting, fraud prevention, security, technical stability, detecting invalid ad traffic, and improving the Games where those interests are not overridden by users’ rights.

Advertising

Our Games display advertisements mediated through Unity LevelPlay. LevelPlay connects the Game with third-party advertising demand sources (such as Unity Ads). These advertising partners process device identifiers, IP addresses, and ad interaction metrics to deliver advertisements, prevent fraud, and limit ad repetition.

Where prior consent is required by regional laws (such as GDPR), personalized advertisements are not requested unless valid consent has been explicitly granted by the user via the consent window. The specific advertising partners available through LevelPlay mediation may change over time.

Consent and Privacy Choices

Where required by applicable law, the Game displays a privacy message provided through Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) before enabling advertising or other processing that requires consent. You can review or change your available consent choices at any time by opening the privacy settings button provided within the Game menu.

You may also limit personalized advertising tracking directly via your device settings (e.g., on Android via Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete/Reset Advertising ID).

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand general usage patterns and improve our Games. Firebase Analytics may process information such as app sessions, device characteristics, app-instance identifiers, and gameplay events.

We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to identify crashes and technical bugs. Crashlytics processes technical diagnostic data, including device details, app state at the moment of failure, and stack traces. We do not transmit names, email addresses, or identifying user inputs to these services.

Data Sharing

Information may be shared with or processed by the third-party advertising, analytics, and operational vendors listed above. Wigimaru does not sell or share names, email addresses, or other information that directly identifies individual players. Some privacy laws (like CCPA/CPRA) use broad definitions of “sale” or “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising that may encompass the automated transmission of advertising identifiers. Where applicable, the Game provides the opt-out mechanism required by law.

Information may also be disclosed where required by applicable law or a valid request from a public authority, or transferred in connection with a sale, merger, restructuring, or transfer of a Game or Wigimaru’s business.

Data Retention and Deletion

Wigimaru does not maintain player accounts or a central database of individual players. Information processed by third-party SDKs is retained according to those providers’ privacy policies and retention practices.

Users may remove locally stored game data by clearing the Game’s application data through their device settings or by uninstalling the Game. This does not necessarily delete information previously processed by third-party service providers.

Data Security

Wigimaru does not operate player accounts or maintain a central database of individual players. Information transmitted by the Games is processed using the security measures provided by Google, Firebase, Unity, and other integrated service providers, including encryption in transit where supported.

International Data Processing

Third-party service providers may process and store information in countries outside your residence (including the United States). These countries may have different data-protection frameworks. The integrated service providers are responsible for applying appropriate cross-border transfer safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) where required.

Children’s Privacy

Our Games are intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and are not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not provide player accounts, request users’ dates of birth, or knowingly collect names, email addresses, or other contact information from children.

Our Games do not independently verify a player’s age. Device settings, parental controls, platform restrictions, and consent choices may limit the information available to third-party services. We also configure our advertising and analytics services in accordance with the audience settings selected for each Game.

Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EU/EEA, California, UK), you may possess legal rights to access, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data. Because Wigimaru does not collect names or emails, we cannot link an email request to specific game-instance data. You can exercise your rights directly by adjusting the in-game consent settings or by resetting/limiting advertising IDs via your device operating system settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our Games, SDK configurations, or legal landscape. The updated version will be hosted on this URL with a revised effective date.
